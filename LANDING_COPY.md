## FAQ — 5 Real Questions a Skeptical CTO Would Ask

**Q1: How do you know what the right plan is for my team?**
We use publicly available pricing from each vendor's official page, verified weekly.
The audit engine checks your seat count against plan thresholds (for example:
ChatGPT Team is priced per seat — for 3 users, Plus is almost always cheaper),
and cross-checks your primary use case against each tool's actual capabilities.
Every recommendation includes a one-sentence reason. You should be able to
disagree with it and tell us why.

**Q2: Is this actually free? What's the catch?**
The audit is completely free. No credit card, no trial, no paywall.
Credex makes money by connecting high-savings teams with discounted AI credits —
the same tools at a lower price, sourced from companies that over-purchased.
If your audit shows significant savings, we'll surface that option. If you're
already spending well, we'll tell you that instead.

**Q3: How current is the pricing data?**
Every number is verified against official vendor pricing pages and timestamped.
AI tool pricing changes frequently — we update the data as part of our publishing
process. The exact sources and verification dates are published in our open-source
repository under `PRICING_DATA.md`.

**Q4: What do you do with my email?**
If you choose to share it: we send one confirmation email with your audit results.
If your savings are significant, a Credex team member may follow up with a
specific offer. We do not sell your data, add you to newsletter lists without
consent, or share your stack information with any third party.

**Q5: My team uses tools not on your list. Can you still audit us?**
The audit covers the most common AI tools used by engineering teams. If your
primary tools are listed, you'll get a useful result. If you're heavily on a
tool we haven't added yet, email us — we add new tools based on demand and
will notify you when yours is supported.
