**Cursor** 

* 
**Available Plans & Pricing:** Individual plans include Hobby (Free) , Pro ($20/mo.) , Pro+ ($60/mo.) , and Ultra ($200/mo.). Business plans include Teams ($40/user/mo.) and Enterprise (Custom Price).


* 
**Number of Seats & Team Size:** The Teams plan provides a shared team context , while the Enterprise tier allows for pooled usage and SCIM seat management.


* **Primary Use Case:** Coding. Features are heavily focused on development, including Tab completions, AI code tracking APIs, and bugbots.



**Claude** 

* 
**Available Plans & Pricing:** Individual tiers are Free , Pro ($17/mo.) , and Max ($100/mo.). Business tiers include Team Standard ($20-$25/seat/mo.) , Team Premium ($100-$125/seat/mo.) , and Enterprise ($20/seat + API usage costs).


* 
**Number of Seats & Team Size:** The Team plan is explicitly designed for team sizes ranging from 5 to 150 members.


* **Primary Use Case:** Mixed. Capabilities span generating code, analyzing text and images, writing, and enterprise search.



**OpenAI** 

* 
**Available Plans & Pricing:** Individual plans include Free , Go ($399) , Plus ($1999) , and Pro ($10699). Business plans include Codex (Pay-as-you-go based on usage) and ChatGPT & Codex (₹1,800/user/month).


* 
**Number of Seats & Team Size:** The Business Codex plan has no fixed seat fee. The ChatGPT & Codex tier is geared towards "startups and growing businesses".


* **Primary Use Case:** Mixed. Use cases cover AI-powered software engineering, deep research, and image creation.



**GitHub** 

* 
**Available Plans & Pricing:** Free ($0 USD per user/month) , Team ($4 USD per user/month) , and Enterprise (Starting at $21 USD per user/month).


* 
**Number of Seats & Team Size:** Plans are scaled for both "individuals and organizations".


* **Primary Use Case:** Coding. Features revolve around repositories, CI/CD minutes, pull requests, and security updates.



**Gemini** 

* 
**Available Plans & Pricing:** Free of charge (₹0 INR/month) , Google AI Plus (₹399 INR/month) , Google AI Pro (₹1,950 INR/month) , and Google AI Ultra.


* 
**Number of Seats & Team Size:** These are positioned as individual plans tied directly to a single Google Account.


* **Primary Use Case:** Mixed. Supports research, writing (via NotebookLM), video/image generation, and coding through Gemini Code Assist.



**v0** 

* 
**Available Plans & Pricing:** Free ($0/month) , Team ($30/user/month) , Business ($100/user/month) , and Enterprise (Custom Pricing).


* 
**Number of Seats & Team Size:** The Team and Business plans are built for shared team collaboration and centralized billing.


* **Primary Use Case:** Coding. Specialized in deploying apps to Vercel, syncing with GitHub, and visual editing.
